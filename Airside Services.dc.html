Contact Us

We offer a full range of ground support services, delivered to the highest safety and operational standards.

Aircraft Handling & Ramp Services

Handling of aircraft on the ground — parking, towing and positioning on remote stands — plus loading and unloading of baggage, cargo and passengers.

Aircraft Fueling

Ground personnel fuel aircraft with the appropriate type and quantity for the next flight, using specialised equipment and following strict safety protocols.

Baggage Handling & Loading

Handling, sorting and loading of baggage and cargo — properly screened, sorted and loaded according to flight schedules and destination requirements.

Passenger Services

Passenger check-in, boarding assistance, wheelchair assistance and other passenger-related services for a smooth and comfortable travel experience.

Aircraft Cleaning & Cabin Services

Cleaning the cabin, restocking supplies and routine cabin maintenance between flights — ensuring cleanliness, comfort and safety for passengers and crew.

Aircraft Maintenance Support

Ground maintenance support — minor repairs, inspections and servicing of systems — plus ground power to maintain essential systems and passenger comfort before take-off.
