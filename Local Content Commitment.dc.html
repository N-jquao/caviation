Local Content Commitment

Century Aviation Ltd is committed to Ghana and the West Africa region for the long term. As a local company, we are dedicated to improving and developing aviation capacity in the oil and gas sectors.

Building lasting aviation capacity in Ghana and West Africa — investing in local people, infrastructure, businesses and communities.

Training local technical staff

At Century, we believe in empowering local talent to excel in the aviation industry. Through comprehensive training programs we nurture the skills of pilots, engineers, maintenance staff and other technical professionals — ensuring the highest standards of safety and quality while contributing to the growth of the local workforce.

Developing local infrastructure

As part of our commitment to sustainable aviation ecosystems, we actively participate in developing local infrastructure. Whether upgrading airport facilities, enhancing air traffic control systems or investing in modernisation projects, we strive to create an environment conducive to safe and efficient air travel across the region.

Support to West African sub-contractors

We recognise the importance of collaboration and partnership in driving progress. That's why we extend our support to West African sub-contractors, fostering mutually beneficial relationships that promote economic growth and innovation. By sourcing goods and services locally, we contribute to the development of local businesses and communities.

Corporate social responsibility

Our responsibility goes beyond business success; it extends to the well-being of the communities we serve. Through our CSR initiatives we engage in projects that address social, environmental and economic challenges — supporting education, promoting environmental sustainability and providing humanitarian aid to leave a lasting legacy of progress and prosperity.
