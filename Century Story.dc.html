Our credentials

Licensed, compliant and built to standard

The journey of Century began with the acquisition of the Air Carrier License (ACL) in 2011, marking our entry into the aviation domain. This was followed by the Airline Operator's License (AOC) in 2013, granted by the Ghana Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA). These milestones are not merely regulatory compliances — they reflect our unwavering commitment to operational excellence and our ambition to redefine Ghana's aviation industry.

Our Air Carrier License allows us to provide Scheduled and Non-Scheduled Services for both Rotary and Fixed Wing operations, as well as On-Demand Rotary and Fixed Wing Charter Services and Aerial Works. We ply Domestic routes, Regional (Africa), and Worldwide for Non-Scheduled and Charter Operations.

Century Aviation is duly registered with the Petroleum Commission, maintains a valid Petroleum Commission Certificate, and is fully compliant with all Petroleum Commission regulations.
